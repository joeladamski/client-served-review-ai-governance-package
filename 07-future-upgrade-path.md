# 07 — Future Upgrade Path

## Phase 1 (Now): Governance Baseline
- Deploy system message, boundaries, and refusal paths.
- Enable moderation queue and basic risk labeling.
- Track safety KPI dashboard manually.

## Phase 2 (Next): Reliability & Analytics
- Add automated policy regression suite.
- Introduce per-intent quality scoring.
- Add sentiment-integrity checks (before vs after draft).

## Phase 3 (Scale): Multi-Location & Multi-Language
- Locale-aware templates for regional tone norms.
- Language-specific moderation rules.
- Franchise-level policy variants with centralized oversight.

## Phase 4 (Enterprise): Compliance & Governance Ops
- Formal incident response runbooks.
- Retention and deletion controls by jurisdiction.
- Quarterly governance review board with change logs.

## Upgrade Principles
- Keep constitutional rules stable; iterate implementation around them.
- Use versioned policy files and changelog entries for traceability.
- Prefer measurable controls over informal prompt tweaks.
